Instructions

  • To start, enter a word into the input box at the top left, then click on the Start button. To add nodes to your graph, click on the "Explore" button on a node.
  • Click and drag a node to move it around the graph.
  • Click on the reset button to start over with a new graph. The Show Info button toggles the info box on the left.
